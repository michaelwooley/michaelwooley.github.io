Drawing With d3.js Part 1: Rectangles

   Michael Wooley
 Sep 5, 2017
d3.js Drawing TableReader

In this post I am going to take a first step towards a sort of drawing app with the d3.js library.⊕ While there is a larger point to this little project, I’m not going to get into it right now. By the end of this series the purpose will come into focus. By the end of this post we’ll have a functioning-but-limited drawing app. In particular, we’ll be able to add rectangles to an svg canvas via a click-and-drag gesture.
